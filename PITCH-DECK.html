Safety Architecture

Building regulatory trust that competitors cannot retrofit

🔐Server-enforced limits — cannot be bypassed client-side
🆔Age verification + parental consent gates for under-16s
⏰Under-16: 2 quests/day, 3 hrs. 16+: 3 quests/day, 5 hrs.
📍35km radius + 9PM-7AM curfew for all teens
💳Escrow payments — teens only paid on confirmed completion
🤖AI moderation — Google Cloud Vision on all uploads
💬Quest-scoped messaging — no open DMs, no social feed
📡Live location sharing with parents during active quests
This safety layer is expensive to build and creates a moat. General-purpose platforms cannot credibly retrofit these protections.
