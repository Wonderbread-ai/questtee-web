Privacy Policy

At Questtee, your privacy is a core part of our safety architecture. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of information required for a safe and functional community marketplace:

  • Account details (name, email, age)
  • Suburbs of residence (never your exact home address)
  • Teenage Questtee profiles (skills, badges, ratings)
  • Quest details (tasks, price, location, photos)

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to facilitate the marketplace, enable safe communication, process payments (via Stripe), and ensure compliance with our safety standards.

3. Sharing & Privacy Controls

Questtees' precise locations are never shared with other users. We never sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties. We only share information with payment processors (Stripe) and as required by law.
