The Journey to your first Quest.

Whether you're looking to earn, looking to help, or making sure your teen is safe—we've simplified every step.

1

Download & Onboard

Grab the Questtee app from the App Store or Google Play. Complete our interactive safety onboarding to learn the rules of the road.

2

Create Your Hero Profile

Pick your avatar, list your skills (mowing, pet care, tech help), and link your Stripe account. If you're under 18, we'll help you get parent sign-off in seconds.

3

Browse & Apply

Check out the real-time quest feed in your neighbourhood. Find something you like? Apply with one tap. No awkward interviews—just show your skills.

4

Complete & Get Paid

Head to the quest location, do a great job, and mark it complete. Once the Quest Giver confirms, funds are released to your account. A 10% platform fee is deducted to support our community safety systems.

1

Parental Consent

When your teenager signs up, you'll receive a one-tap consent request via email. You're always the gatekeeper.

2

Safety Guardrails

Our system automatically blocks quests during school hours and late at night. We also hide your home's exact location from potential Quest Givers.

3

Full Visibility

Access your dedicated Parent Dashboard to see exactly what quests your teen is doing, how much they're earning, and who they're helping.

1

Post a Quest

Describe the help you need, set a fair price (between $5–$50), and choose a time that works for you. It takes under 3 minutes.

2

Review Applications

Browse profiles of local, motivated teens who've applied. See their ratings, badges, and history before you choose who to invite over.

3

Job Done, Effortless Pay

Once the quest is finished, confirm it in the app. Payment is handled automatically via escrow, with a flat $0.50 listing fee added to your total. No need to worry about change or cash.
