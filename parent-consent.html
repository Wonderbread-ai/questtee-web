Parent Guardian Consent

Our goal is to create a space that parents feel comfortable allowing their children to use. For all users under 16, a guardian's consent is mandatory before they can apply for or accept any quest on the platform.

How It Works

  • When your teen registers as a Questtee over 12 and under 16, they must provide your contact email.
  • You'll receive an automated notification with their profile details.
  • A single tap in the email grants permanent or temporary consent to use the app.
  • You can revoke this consent at any time via your Parent Dashboard.

What You're Agreeing To

By providing consent, you acknowledge that your teenager is allowed to:

  • Browse local community tasks on the Questtee platform.
  • Communicate with Quest Givers through our secure messaging system.
  • Travel within a 10km radius of their registered suburb to complete quests.
  • Receive secure electronic payments from Quest Givers via Stripe.
